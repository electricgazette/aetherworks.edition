A New Test for Acidity & Alkalinity

Professor Amelia Kersey, of the Chemical Section, has laid before the Fellows a dainty contrivance...

By steeping fine paper in extracts of certain vegetable dyes—among them the juice of red cabbage...

In a demonstration that delighted the assembled savants, a series of household fluids were presented...

“We may now ascertain, in the very scullery itself,” Professor Kersey declared...
